How We Work with Communities

  • Start with Community Engagement: We collaborate with local leaders, schools, and groups to understand needs, raise awareness, and build trust.
  • Deploy Solar-Powered Mesh Networks: We install Wi-Fi stations using mesh topology, which connects multiple access points to create strong, flexible, and community-managed internet coverage.
  • Train Local Youth: We hire and train young people from the community to install, maintain, and support the network‚Äîbuilding skills and creating local jobs.
  • Offer Digital Identity & Financial Tools: Through our platform, people can create secure digital IDs and access mobile banking, government services, and economic opportunities.
  • Support Inclusion & Literacy: We run local workshops on digital skills, financial literacy, and internet safety‚Äîespecially for women, youth, and small businesses.
  • Partner for Impact: We work with banks, telecoms, NGOs, and innovators to connect services and scale sustainably.

‚ö†Ô∏è Risks & Mitigation

We have identified key risks and devised strategies to ensure the success of MTANDAO POINT:

  • Power Outages: Mitigation: Solar-powered system with battery backups and mesh routing for reliability.
  • ISP Delays: Mitigation: Established MoUs with multiple ISPs, regulatory incentives, and alternative partnerships to ensure bandwidth supply.
  • Community Trust: Mitigation: Engage local leaders, peer educators, and a demo-first approach to build confidence in the solution.
  • Funding Constraints: Mitigation: Diversified funding streams from users, grants, and social investors, along with targeted fundraising campaigns.
  • Privacy & Data Security: Mitigation: Implement a blockchain-based system for data protection and local data governance to safeguard user privacy.
