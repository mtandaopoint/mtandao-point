MTANDAO POINT

Affordable Internet, Digital Identity, and Financial Access for Rural Tanzania

Bridging the digital divide with solar-powered Wi-Fi, blockchain identity, and mobile financial tools for underserved communities.

Community-Powered Mesh Network

Solar-powered mesh Wi-Fi with TP-Link routers provides decentralized, resilient connectivity in rural areas.

Blockchain-Based Digital Identity

We use Hyperledger Fabric for verifiable mobile digital identities linked to essential services.

Financial Inclusion

Link digital IDs with M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and NMB Bank for seamless payments, savings, and microloans.

Live Deployment

Our pilot at Ng'ong'ona serves over 50 users with real-time bandwidth monitoring via Omada Controller.
