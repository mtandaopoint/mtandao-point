ABOUT MTANDAO POINT

Who We Are

MTANDAO POINT is a community-based network providing affordable internet access to underserved communities. Through a shared ownership model, local residents contribute to the network's sustainability, gaining access to vital online resources. The collected fees are reinvested to support community development, empowering people to thrive and grow through technology.

Our Impact

We are transforming communities by providing digital access to education, business opportunities, and financial services. Our network strengthens local economies, connects individuals to global markets, and fosters innovation in rural areas.
