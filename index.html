MTANDAO POINT Concept Note

Executive Summary

MTANDAO POINT is a community-driven initiative aimed at bridging the digital divide by offering affordable and reliable internet access. By leveraging wireless technology and a shared economic model, we empower individuals and businesses with digital opportunities.

Mission

To make internet access affordable and accessible to underserved communities, fostering economic growth and digital literacy.

Vision

To become the leading wireless community network café in Tanzania, enabling digital inclusion for all.

Key Features:

  • Low-cost, high-speed internet services
  • Community-driven ownership model
  • Support for education, businesses, and entrepreneurs
  • Scalable infrastructure for rural and urban areas
  • Digital literacy training programs
  • Smart partnerships with local businesses and organizations

Our Services

  • Affordable Wi-Fi access
  • Community Internet Cafés
  • Digital Training & Support
  • Tech Solutions for Small Businesses

Our Impact

MTANDAO POINT is actively working to enhance digital literacy and internet accessibility within the community. Through dedicated efforts, more individuals and businesses are gaining access to the digital world, fostering growth and opportunities.
