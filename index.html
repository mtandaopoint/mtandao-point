About Us

MTANDAO POINT is dedicated to providing affordable and reliable community wireless internet access, empowering underserved communities through digital inclusion.

MTANDAO POINT Concept Note

Executive Summary: MTANDAO POINT is a community-driven initiative aimed at bridging the digital divide by offering affordable and reliable internet access. By leveraging wireless technology and a shared economic model, we empower individuals and businesses with digital opportunities.

Mission: To make internet access affordable and accessible to underserved communities, fostering economic growth and digital literacy.

Vision: To become the leading wireless community network café in Tanzania, enabling digital inclusion for all.

Key Features:

  • Low-cost, high-speed internet services
  • Community-driven ownership model
  • Support for education, businesses, and entrepreneurs
  • Scalable infrastructure for rural and urban areas
  • Digital literacy training programs
  • Smart partnerships with local businesses and organizations

Our Services

  • Affordable Wi-Fi access
  • Community Internet Cafés
  • Digital Training & Support
  • Tech Solutions for Small Businesses

Our Impact

MTANDAO POINT has helped bridge the digital divide by providing internet access to thousands of users, fostering entrepreneurship, and supporting digital education programs. Our work has empowered students, job seekers, and small businesses with the tools they need to thrive in a digital economy.
